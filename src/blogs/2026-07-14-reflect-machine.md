To be honest, I don't even remember why I decided to release this game on Steam. Maybe it was the best rated one or
seemed like the most doable one. In the end it was just another game jam prototype in around 2021, I think. In
hindsight, I should have select a more interesting genre than a classic puzzle platformer. I had some options
available at that time and should have picked better. But not much I can do now.

I had to start somewhere and either way the first commercial game wouldn't be much of a success anyway. At least I 
learned a lot along the way which was probably more than I expected. There is many things to learn about the Steam 
Marketplace and how to increase your visibility there etc. I'm no expert in that area, so I just reference to one that 
is: https://howtomarketagame.com/. He helped me a lot in that regard as I had absolutely no experience in that area.

But at that time, I don't think I actually did everything he recommended or haven't gone through everything yet. It
has been a while already, so I might not remember the stuff correctly. At least I did most of it in my second game
and it has been performing much better.

Anyway that's as much as I can remember about this game and the development process around it. I would just see this
first game of mine as a learning experience and that maybe not every game has to be released on Steam which I should
have thought about for my upcoming game, but it's a little bit too late for that...
